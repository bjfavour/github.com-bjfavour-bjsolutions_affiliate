About B&J Solutions

B&J Solutions is an innovative affiliate marketing platform designed to help individuals earn from promoting real consumer products and services. Our platform is simple, transparent, and empowering.

Affiliate Features

Affiliates can:

  • Sign up with a unique email or phone number, full name, and password
  • Access a secure login portal to track commissions in real-time
  • Update their profile with bank account details
  • Change their password at any time
  • Submit cashout requests for payment
